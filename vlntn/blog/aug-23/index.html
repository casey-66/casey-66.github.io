August 23rd

Two broken animals dance in the starlight
Night after night after night
A clearing in the woods bathed in the light of their love
A sky full of stars and a ring of brilliant blooming branches
So beautiful, so brilliant, so blooming


Our roadkill bleeding out on the wild grass
Mangled gore intertwined
One pain, one stomped carcass of two heads and eight legs
Dancing in the starlight
So tragic, so manic, so frantic


Do they know their blood will never be separate?
Never
Beady, pale eyes locked together
Chipped teeth and flesh-buried claws
The living creatures of the forest ran away long ago
So beautiful, so beautiful, so beautiful
