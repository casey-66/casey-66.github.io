December 3rd

My Valentine does not understand Jackie very well.

Jackie is scared. All of the time in the pit of her stomach is a terror my Valentine could never comprehend.

There is a peace in being nothing. It is really a blessing to be depressed the same amount every day.

Why break that? To grow is to push past your container and fall across the ground to be eaten.

The future doesn’t exist, my Valentine.
