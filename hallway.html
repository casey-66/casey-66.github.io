There is a world outside of these walls.

Bathed in light and joy and Love you can't even understand.

You have seen it in a dream.

Some days it feels like these walls were made just for you.

Others, you for the walls.

Don't get your hopes up, kid.

There is no grand escape.

This is what you've earned.

What you really want.

Fresh air will dry out your soft shell.
